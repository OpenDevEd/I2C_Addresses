# 0x00 - 0x0F

### 0x00 - Reserved - General Call Address
### 0x01 - Reserved for CBUS Compatibility
### 0x02 - Reserved for I2C-compatible Bus Variants
### 0x03 - Reserved for Future Use
### 0x04, 0x05, 0x06, 0x07 - Reserved for Hs-mode Controller
